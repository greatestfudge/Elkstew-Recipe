<h1>Elkstew for cowboys</h1>
<p> So you have searched for a recipe on how to make stew of delicious meat such as elk or <a href="https://sv.wikipedia.org/wiki/%C3%84lgar"> aelg </a href> as we would pronounce it in swedish. To begin with either aquire elkmeat from the market or go out hunting yourself. Preferebly with a friend or more.
